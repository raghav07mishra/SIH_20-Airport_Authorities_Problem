Our Story

Since a flight lands and again takeoff in between the time each and every thing is under the control of ground force,involving a lot of checks even in a second so as to provide safe flight to passenger however everything is paperwork and it's record are being mantained so chances fo some incupency is still left! Checks starting from as soon as a flight land like touch down angle, wind pressure to on/off board eachand every small to complex process is under ground force and airport officer so, working digitally will provide them flexibility as well as eliminates the need to file the records meanwhile each and everything is on the internal airport servers! In past there are lot off accidents which can be prevented by such track where digital means passenger's security.

Our AIM!!

As per as the current airport authorities and ground forces policies in between the landing and again takeoff of a flight each and everything is involved as paperwork, our team is focusing on the same issue to provide CNS/ATM faculties as per as required on a digital background!, which will really enhance the security of passengers travelling in a particular flight, as well increase the efficiency of the airport by digitally transacting between ground force and ATC.
